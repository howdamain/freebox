Common questions

  • Manage or cancel your subscription: open the Settings tab in the app and tap “Manage Subscription,” or go to Settings › Apple Account › Subscriptions on your device.
  • Restore a purchase: Settings tab › “Restore Purchases.”
  • Delete your account: Settings tab › “Delete Account.”
  • Report a listing: open any listing and tap “Report this listing.”
