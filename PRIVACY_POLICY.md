# Freebox — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-16_

Freebox ("we", "the app") helps you discover free local items and track resale potential. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

> **Hosting:** the public-facing version lives in [`web/privacy.html`](web/privacy.html) (+ [`web/delete-account.html`](web/delete-account.html)) and is published via `.github/workflows/pages.yml` (GitHub Pages). After enabling Pages, put the live URLs in the Play listing + `strings.xml` (`privacy_policy_url`, `data_deletion_url`). This `.md` is the source/reference copy.

## Information we collect
- **Account:** your email address (for sign-in via Supabase Auth).
- **Location (ZIP code):** the ZIP code you enter during onboarding, used to determine which area's listings to show you and to schedule listing collection for that area. We do **not** collect continuous background location.
- **App activity:** items you save to your Vault, profit alerts you create, and listings you claim.

## How we use it
- To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
- To show free listings near the ZIP you provide and to operate your watchlist, alerts, and claims.
- We do **not** sell your personal information or share it with advertisers.

## Listing data
Listings shown in the app are aggregated from publicly available third-party marketplaces. We display item details (title, photo, approximate location, estimated value); we do not collect or store personal data about the original posters beyond what is needed to show the listing.

## Data storage & security
Your data is stored in our Supabase (PostgreSQL) backend, protected by row-level security so each user can access only their own records. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

## Subscriptions
Freebox offers a paid subscription. Payments are processed by Google Play Billing; we do not receive or store your payment-card details.

## Your choices & data deletion
- **Delete your account in-app:** Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data (profile, saved items, alerts, claims, subscription record).
- You may also request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

## Children
Freebox is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
